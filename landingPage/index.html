Transform your inventory into safer shelves

In Vancouver alone, there are over 238 pharmacies managing thousands of medications every day. Yet outdated inventory processes cause nearly 10% of products to expire before being sold.

Expired medications are not regular waste. They must be incinerated, and when mishandled, they can pollute soil, drainage systems, and surrounding environments—creating serious environmental and compliance risks.

ShelfSafe helps pharmacies prevent expired medications, reduce waste, and ensure stored products are always available without manual shelf checks.

How it works?

Subscribe →

Shelf Safe connects inventory data into a single dashboard, continuously tracking medication expiry dates and stock levels, no manual shelf checks required.

Automated alerts notify pharmacy staff when medications are approaching expiry or running low, so action can be taken before products become a risk.

Shelf Safe continuously monitors inventory, providing automated alerts for upcoming expirations and low-stock medications. This allows pharmacy staff to act proactively and guarantee timely access to essential products.

By preventing medication expirations, Shelf Safe reduces unnecessary disposal and the emissions associated with incineration. Pharmacies can maintain responsible waste management and support sustainability goals.

Early detection of at-risk inventory prevents product loss, lowers disposal costs, and improves overall stock efficiency. Shelf Safe enables pharmacies to make data‑driven decisions that protect margins and streamline operations.
